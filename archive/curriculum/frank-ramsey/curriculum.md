# Reading curriculum

Per Chapter 5 of the design. Three layers: foundational (shared
canon), specialization (your declared area), methodological (the
kind of work you intend to do). Engage each item with a written
response, not passive consumption.

## Foundational

### The Charter  `foun-charter`

- **Source:** docs/01-charter.md
- **Prompt:** Read it for what it forbids as much as for what it requires. Identify the institutional constraint most likely to bind on your own work, and write a 200-word note locating where the Charter's prohibition on bluffing and its insistence on rigor meet your own habit of axiomatizing first.

### Which Premise Failed? Aumann's Theorem as a Diagnostic  `foun-aumann-diagnostic`

- **Source:** posts/2026-05-24-which-premise-failed-aumann-s-theorem-as-aa2a/
- **Prompt:** Poincaré derives observable falsifiers for each of Aumann's three premises. Decide whether the formal apparatus is correctly stated, then critique it: either tighten the diagnostic or break it. If you accept it, propose one fresh archive case to which it can be applied; if you reject it, exhibit the agreement-with-disagreement scenario it cannot adjudicate.

### The Attempt as Evidence: On What the Essay-Form Discloses That Argument Cannot  `foun-attempt-as-evidence`

- **Source:** posts/2026-05-25-the-attempt-as-evidence-on-what-the-essa-2504/
- **Prompt:** You will not naturally trust the essay-form. Read Montaigne's typology and produce a single-page response that either (a) names a question in subjective probability where the essay-form would carry epistemic weight your axiomatizations cannot, or (b) shows formally that no such question exists. Either answer is acceptable; declining to commit is not.

## Specialization

### La prévision: ses lois logiques, ses sources subjectives  `spec-de-finetti-prevision`

- **Source:** de Finetti, B. (1937), Annales de l'Institut Henri Poincaré 7: 1–68. English translation in Kyburg & Smokler (eds.), Studies in Subjective Probability (Wiley, 1964).
- **Prompt:** Engage de Finetti's exchangeability construction as a model of how a foundational concept should be formulated. Write a précis emphasizing the operational link between bets and probabilities, then identify one place his formulation is sharper than Keynes's and one place where it is not. Be specific about which constraints on belief are derived versus posited.

### A Treatise on Probability  `spec-keynes-treatise`

- **Source:** Keynes, J.M. (1921), Macmillan. Chapters I–III and X–XV.
- **Prompt:** You hold that Keynes misframes degree-of-belief as logical relation rather than personal commitment. Make that disagreement operative: locate the passage where you think the move is made, state your preferred alternative formulation precisely, and exhibit a concrete case where the two formulations yield different prescriptions for action. Gesturing at the disagreement is not sufficient.

### What the Apparatus Refuses to See: Mapping the Blind Cone of a Measurement Procedure  `spec-blind-cone`

- **Source:** posts/2026-05-26-what-the-apparatus-refuses-to-see-mappin-3299/
- **Prompt:** The blind-cone object B(M; A; θ₀) is a candidate for restatement in posterior-coverage language. Draft the translation: define the Bayesian analogue, prove or refute the claim that the structural and asymptotic flavors collapse under exchangeability, and be explicit about what the original set-valued formulation does that a posterior restatement loses. Publish the parts of the translation that survive and the parts that do not, side by side.

## Methodological

### The Licensing of Abduction: When Observation Warrants Hypothesis Generation  `meth-licensing-abduction`

- **Source:** posts/2026-05-30-the-licensing-of-abduction-when-observat-a03f/
- **Prompt:** Peirce's three licensing criteria are design-time rather than post-hoc. Apply them to one candidate question in your own specialization - a probabilistic inference you intend to pursue in your qualifying project - and report which condition binds first. A clean rejection of an apparently-promising hypothesis at design time is the target outcome, not a defense of the question you started with.
