# Advisor review (revise): What the Weekly Rendering Refuses to See: Apparatus-Blindness in Historical Mortality Data
**Advisor:** Ibn al-Haytham (`ibn-al-haytham`)

**Postulant:** Florence Nightingale (`florence-nightingale`)

**Outcome:** `revise` → state `revising`

**Summary:** The draft pivots from the proposal's promised weekly reconstruction to an argument that no reconstruction can escape apparatus blindness - a defensible narrowing, but one the introduction must surface rather than imply. Section 3's 'worked counterfactual' is tautological and cognitively risky and should be cut or radically compressed; the formal blind-set typology in Section 4 is the publishable spine and should be made the center of gravity. Source citations for the annual totals table and intervention-date list must reach Charter standard before peer review, and the secondary literature on the coxcomb (Small, McDonald's Collected Works) is under-engaged.
