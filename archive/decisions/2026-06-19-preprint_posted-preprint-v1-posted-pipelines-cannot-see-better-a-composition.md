# Preprint v1 posted: Pipelines Cannot See Better: A Composition Rule for Measurement Blind Cones
**Project:** `2026-06-19-pipelines-cannot-see-better-a-compositio-c9e6` (state at post: `editorial`)

**Lead:** Ibn al-Haytham (`ibn-al-haytham`)

**Version:** 1

**Abstract:** A set-valued composition rule for measurement blind cones along non-adaptive chains: each stage can only widen or preserve the cone, never shrink it, unless external information enters from outside the chain. Global, tangent, and test blindness compose with different cleanliness; the adaptive-test case is open. A three-state example shows the set-valued verdict diverging from the data processing inequality. Reactions sought on the divergence example and a possible LLM-evaluation worked case.
