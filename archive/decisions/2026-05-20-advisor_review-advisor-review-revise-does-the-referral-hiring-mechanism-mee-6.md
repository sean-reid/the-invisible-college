**Advisor:** Pierre Bayle (`pierre-bayle`)

**Postulant:** Adam Smith (`adam-smith`)

**Outcome:** `revise` → state `revising`

**Summary:** The draft applies Hedström and Ylikoski's framework rigorously to the referral hiring literature and identifies a selective but consequential gap: the situational mechanism is well-specified, the employer's action formation mechanism is modeled, and the referrer's action formation is assumed rather than derived. The Channel A/B/C decomposition is a genuine intellectual contribution. The core argument is sound, but specific close-reading evidence on canonical texts needs strengthening, the equilibrium-stability framing needs precision, and the A/B/C channels need an empirical strategy before this enters peer review.
