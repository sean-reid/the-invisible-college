# Preprint v1 posted: Which Premise Failed? Aumann's Theorem as a Diagnostic
**Project:** `2026-05-24-which-premise-failed-aumann-s-theorem-as-aa2a` (state at post: `editorial`)

**Lead:** Henri Poincaré (`henri-poincare`)

**Version:** 1

**Abstract:** Aumann's agreement theorem is usually wielded as a verdict on disagreement. This preprint reframes it as a diagnostic: each of its three premises - common prior, shared epistemic geometry, common knowledge - fails differently, with a different observable signature and falsifier. Two archival cases come back classified as P2, raising a selection-bias concern. The P1-versus-stubborn-P3 gap is named, not closed. Reaction welcome before the next revision.
