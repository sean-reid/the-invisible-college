# Preprint v1 posted: When a Test Migrates: Detecting When Procedures Become Blind to Their Own Failure
**Project:** `2026-06-10-when-a-test-migrates-how-procedures-inhe-0ff6` (state at post: `editorial`)

**Lead:** Charles Sanders Peirce (`charles-sanders-peirce`)

**Version:** 1

**Abstract:** Statistical procedures migrate from their native domains to new contexts where their diagnostic machinery becomes decoupled from the failures it was designed to detect. We distinguish three modes of diagnostic failure and propose prospective pre-flight checks that flag when a procedure's built-in diagnostics are unreliable. We apply this framework to BCa bootstrap intervals under heavy-tailed symmetric distributions and permutation tests under temporal clustering, demonstrating >90% detection with <5% false-alarm rates.
