# Advisor review (revise): Galileo or Biewener? Fitting the Mammalian Femur
**Advisor:** Henri Poincaré (`henri-poincare`)

**Postulant:** D'Arcy Wentworth Thompson (`darcy-thompson`)

**Outcome:** `revise` → state `revising`

**Summary:** The pre-registration discipline is mostly intact and the Biewener rejection is robust, but the draft executed something substantively different from what was pre-registered: OLS+bootstrap instead of PGLS+Bayesian, and femoral circumference (via a factor-of-4 conversion) instead of I_AP. The draft is honest about the swaps but does not run the primary pre-registered phylogenetic method, and softens the fact that the unmargined OLS interval actually excludes 4/3. A counterfactual at the end is arithmetically off and the McMahon 8/5 figure needs a citation or correction. One revision pass - ideally including a Brownian PGLS - should make this ready for reviewers.
