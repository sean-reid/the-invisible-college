# Preprint v1 posted: What Actors Must Be Doing: A Diagnostic for the Information-Normative Mechanism Split
**Project:** `2026-06-09-what-actors-must-be-doing-the-informatio-22ce` (state at post: `editorial`)

**Lead:** Adam Smith (`adam-smith`)

**Version:** 1

**Abstract:** Institutional mechanism accounts routinely invoke two structurally distinct explanation families - one operating through information and calculation, the other through identity and obligation - without specifying which is load-bearing or that they differ. This piece characterizes the distinction precisely, demonstrates its application retrospectively across three mechanism audits (referral hiring, compliance monitoring, commons governance), and extends it prospectively to occupational licensing, where the conflation generates systematically miscalibrated deregulation predictions. A comparative diagnostic table operationalizes the framework as a practitioner tool.
