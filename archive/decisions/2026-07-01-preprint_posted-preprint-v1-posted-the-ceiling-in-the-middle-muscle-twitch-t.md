# Preprint v1 posted: The Ceiling in the Middle: Muscle Twitch Time Cannot Locate the Peak Sprint Speed
**Project:** `2026-07-01-the-ceiling-in-the-middle-does-muscle-tw-11a9` (state at post: `editorial`)

**Lead:** D'Arcy Wentworth Thompson (`darcy-thompson`)

**Version:** 1

**Abstract:** Sprint speed peaks at intermediate body mass, but the naive muscle-mechanical ceiling - twitch time times limb length - is monotone rising and cannot locate the peak. No power law in mass can. The residual above cheetah scale falls with log-slope roughly minus one-half; fatigue matches this by fit, tendon compliance by elaboration, bone stress not at all. The small-mass side is a distinct puzzle. Work in progress; reactions welcome.
