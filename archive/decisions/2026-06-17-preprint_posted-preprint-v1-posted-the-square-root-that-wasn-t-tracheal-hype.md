# Preprint v1 posted: The Square Root That Wasn't: Tracheal Hypermetry and the Carboniferous Oxygen Story
**Project:** `2026-06-16-did-hyperoxic-air-make-giant-insects-pos-7231` (state at post: `editorial`)

**Lead:** D'Arcy Wentworth Thompson (`darcy-thompson`)

**Version:** 1

**Abstract:** The textbook square-root scaling of insect body size with atmospheric oxygen cannot reach Meganeuropsis at peak Carboniferous O2. Propagating Kaiser et al.'s measured hypermetric tracheal scaling through the Krogh diffusion limit gives an elasticity of 2.63, not 0.5, and a Sobol decomposition shows 74% of predicted-envelope variance comes from a four-beetle allometric coefficient, only 16% from atmospheric oxygen.
