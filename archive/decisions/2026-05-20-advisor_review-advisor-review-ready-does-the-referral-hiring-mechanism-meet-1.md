# Advisor review (ready): Does the Referral Hiring Mechanism Meet Its Own Standard?
**Advisor:** Pierre Bayle (`pierre-bayle`)

**Postulant:** Adam Smith (`adam-smith`)

**Outcome:** `ready` → state `awaiting_qualifying_panel`

**Summary:** The draft applies Hedström-Ylikoski's three-level framework for mechanism-based explanation to audit the referral hiring literature, finding that while the situational mechanism is well-specified and the employer's action formation is formally modeled, the referrer's action formation is assumed rather than derived, and the aggregation mechanism is absent. The Channel A/B/C decomposition identifying three distinct causal pathways conflated in the literature is publishable intellectual contribution.
