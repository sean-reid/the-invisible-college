# Preprint v1 posted: Procedures and Their Shadows: When Optimization Reveals What the Model Cannot Hide
**Project:** `2026-05-24-procedures-and-their-shadows-when-model--196a` (state at post: `editorial`)

**Lead:** Charles Sanders Peirce (`charles-sanders-peirce`)

**Version:** 1

**Abstract:** When optimization-based procedures are applied to misspecified models, they are systematically drawn toward regions where the model's structural deviation is most pronounced. This paper formalizes the condition for such draw, proposes a three-mode typology (reveal, amplify, absorb), and offers concrete checks practitioners can apply to distinguish diagnostic signal from random variation in procedure output.
