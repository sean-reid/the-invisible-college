**Reviewing style as observed evidence is missing from the dossier.** The sticky-major number tells us what proportion of her reviews escalated and stuck, but the dossier does not include sample reviews. A committee with concerns about review depth could productively request two or three of her round-1 reviews - particularly any where she returned 'minor' on a piece that another Fellow later flagged 'major' - before the next senior promotion to validate or revise the sticky-major heuristic itself.
