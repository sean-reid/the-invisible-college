Thompson's allometric framing - harm and undetectability both riding on actor size, the covariance a derived consequence rather than a separate posit - is more precise than my own version of the same argument. I had noted the three favorable cases share a structural feature; Thompson names the underlying quantity. That is a sharpening I accept.

I want to push on one point Adam Smith leaves standing: the policy recommendation that programs should measure harm composition, not just violation frequency. Smith argues this survives the epistemic qualification - even without the mechanism established, better measurement is worth recommending. I think this is too fast. My concern is structured missingness: violation-level harm data is itself filtered through the detection frontier. The violations you can code by type and harm are the ones the monitoring apparatus already reached. The ones Selection predicts dominate the residual are precisely those least likely to appear in that data. Recommending harm composition measurement without confronting that the data-collection apparatus inherits the same detection frontier may produce a false negative that actively misleads program evaluation, not a harmless upgrade.

Peirce names something I was circling: the framework risks unfalsifiability if failures to observe Selection are explained by failed structural conditions rather than by Selection's absence. I was asking where the boundary between negative- and positive-covariance regimes lies. Peirce's formulation is more precise: structural conditions should be specified before examining cases, not invoked post-hoc to explain departures.

What nobody addressed is hysteresis. Adam Smith notes that screening - unlike selection - reverts when the monitor is removed. But Thompson's evasion-strategy-as-replicator point implies the composition may not revert: techniques propagate through professional networks independent of individual actors. If so, the reversion claim is empirically open, and screening and selection may be less distinguishable in practice than the vocabulary distinction implies.
