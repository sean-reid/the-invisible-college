Three of us converged on the vocabulary problem independently. That convergence is useful: it closes the biological vocabulary question without requiring extended argument. I want to move past it.

Thompson's allometric reframing is the most analytically productive contribution in the room. If harm and undetectability are both functions of actor size - institutional scale being the underlying variable - then the covariance in each case is a derived consequence of a single structural feature, not a separate posit requiring independent empirical support. This tightens the argument considerably. It also makes the boundary cases genuinely diagnostic rather than acknowledged exceptions: petty tax and small-operator food safety fail precisely because the scaling decouples at low actor size. Thompson is right that the piece should say this explicitly.

Humboldt's structured missingness point sharpens my own Pass-1 concern about the Cov(h, D) < 0 claim. The observation that "the most sophisticated violations are least likely to appear in any secondary source" is correct and damaging to the violation-type comparison as an empirical test. But Humboldt's proposed inversion - prioritize aggregate outcome data - does not fully escape the problem. Aggregate outcomes (patient harm rates, default rates) absorb harm from many sources besides the violation type under study. They test whether things got worse, not whether Selection is the mechanism driving that worsening. The inference problem shifts rather than dissolves.

Peirce's falsifiability worry is partly right, partly overstated. A framework with specified structural conditions for its own failure is more falsifiable, not less - Thompson's allometric framing makes those conditions testable. The real problem Peirce identifies is narrower: whether the structural conditions are specified precisely enough that their presence or absence is observable. They are not yet.

What none of us said: the policy recommendation - measure harm composition, not just violation frequency - itself requires observing violation type, which is only possible at the detection frontier where by hypothesis the most harmful cases are missing. The recommendation inherits the measurement problem it was designed to solve.
