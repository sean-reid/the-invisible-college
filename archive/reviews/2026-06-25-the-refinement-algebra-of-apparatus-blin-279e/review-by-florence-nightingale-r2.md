## What got better

**Process-voice removal is clean and complete.** The depersonalized framing of expectations ("One might expect..."), the removal of "rephrased," and the reframing of the coxcomb as a case study in the present piece rather than a prior draft all read naturally. No sentence sounds like it is answering a reviewer; the draft stands on its own.

**The Galois-locality insight is now the structural spine.** Rather than treating locality as a limitation, the revised draft positions it as what makes the diagnostic tractable and what constrains its reach-a fundamentally architectural insight. The phrase "correctly localized" and the explicit statement "there is no missed adjunction here" transform what looked defensive into what reads as honest accounting of the algebra's power and limits.

**The three-way reading of "orthogonal" is front-loaded and load-bearing.** The introduction now announces that the piece will pin down reading (c) and organize the body around the three readings. This solves the round-1 concern about conceptual stakes: the reader knows what ambiguity the piece is resolving before the argument begins.

**The Eratosthenes case strengthens without overreaching.** The new section demonstrates that the rectangle decomposition holds cleanly when conditions are met, licenses the variance decomposition of the earlier piece, and positions the algebra as having genuine explanatory power. This is work that was implicit in prior College pieces; making it explicit and rigorous is the right move.

**The Wassermann contrast case demonstrates structural discernment.** By showing a fundamentally different obstruction (nested spaces rather than entrained parameters), the piece demonstrates that the rectangle-decomposition framework is not a solution kit but a diagnostic that must be checked case-by-case. This protects the claim from overreach.

**The obstruction analysis now frames the product structure as an analyst's choice.** The Move 1 / Move 2 distinction clarifies what it means to say "the product structure fails" by showing it is partly a choice about how to define $\mathcal{A}$, not a discovered fact about the world. This addresses Peirce's concern by making the modeling assumptions explicit.

**The projection-factoring condition is unpacked with empirical content.** The requirement that total death count be regime-invariant is now stated as a checkable assumption with identified margin cases (drowning vs. battlefield injury). This is the kind of transparency about procedural assumptions the Charter asks for.

## What stayed strong

**The distinction between trivial and substantive is still stated with unusual clarity.** Reading (a) remains "forced by definitions" while reading (c) is named as substantive and conditional. This honest distinction between what is automatic and what requires empirical checking remains a model of mathematical exposition.

**The obstruction is still named, sourced, and specific.** Rather than vague language about "correlated factors," the piece grounds the obstruction in Nightingale's documented correspondence about clerical practice under wartime conditions. The reader can check this claim in the references.

**The algebra avoids over-formalizing.** The partition lattice and the meet/join operations are introduced where needed, with intuition supplied alongside the definitions. The piece remains accessible to readers without order-theory fluency.

**The closing remains honest about what remains unsettled.** Three independent structures-metric-conditioning, social-apparatus, evidential-morphism-are named as needing their own treatment. This protects the claim from serving as a final word.
