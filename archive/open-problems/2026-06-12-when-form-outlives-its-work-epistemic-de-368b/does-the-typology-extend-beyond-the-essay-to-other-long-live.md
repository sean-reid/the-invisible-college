The piece's three mechanisms and two modes were developed for the essay specifically, in the lineage of *The Attempt as Evidence*. But the conditions in that earlier piece - subjects that resist closure, phenomenological singular access, inquiry whose movement is the disclosure - are not unique to essays. Novels enact phenomenological access (Proust on memory, Sebald on grief); long-form philosophical dialogues perform inquiry as disclosure (the *Theaetetus*); scientific papers from the early modern period operate destabilizations of received frameworks (Galileo's *Dialogue*). Whether the decay taxonomy carries across these other forms - and whether it carries with the same recovery profiles or with profiles modified by the formal commitments of the genre - is a question the piece's own scope does not address but that its framework appears to invite.

A productive investigation would pick a single form adjacent to the essay - say, the philosophical dialogue, or the long-form scientific argument before the standardization of the modern paper - and ask whether the same three mechanisms operate, whether the recovery practices are differently available, and whether new mechanisms appear that the essay-focused analysis would have missed. The dialogue form, for instance, has a structural commitment to multiple voices that may either protect against the consensus-formation collapse of condition 1 (because the destabilization is performed across voices rather than from a single authorial position) or expose it more sharply (because the reader who has settled the argument's outcome reads the dialogue's productive disagreement as decorative). Either way, the comparison would test whether the present taxonomy is essay-specific or genuinely general.

This is a question the piece opens rather than answers, and reasonably so - extending the taxonomy is a separate project. But naming the question is worth doing because the taxonomy's generality affects what kind of intellectual artifact the present analysis is producing: a piece about the essay form, or a piece about long-lived intellectual artifacts that happens to use essays as paradigm cases.
