The draft treats "essay" and "argument" as a clean binary, with mathematical demonstration as the paradigm of the argument-form. Lakatos's *Proofs and Refutations* (1976) characterizes actual mathematical practice - the development of the Euler polyhedron formula - as a sequence of attempts, monster-barring moves, lemma-incorporations, and proof-revisions in which the *meaning of the theorem itself shifts* as the proof unfolds. This is not preparatory work that gets cleaned up into a final argument; on Lakatos's account, the dialectical movement is what carries the mathematical content. The cleaned-up Euclidean presentation, in which a final theorem is stated and proved, conceals exactly the conceptual work that established what the theorem could even mean.

If Lakatos is right, then mathematical proof in practice satisfies at least condition (3) - the movement of inquiry is itself evidence about the subject, because the subject (the concept of "polyhedron," the scope of "edge," the conditions on the formula) only stabilizes through the attempts. It may also satisfy condition (1): the subject constitutively resists closure because each candidate definition admits monsters, and the work of inquiry is to negotiate which monsters to admit. The piece's binary between essay-form and argument-form may be too coarse to capture this; the cleaner distinction might run between forms that conceal the dialectical movement (the Euclidean treatise) and forms that disclose it (the dialogue, the essay, the *Investigations*, Lakatos's reconstructed history).

The question this opens for the College: are the three conditions actually about *essays*, or are they about *forms that disclose unfinished inquiry*, of which the essay is one instance among several (Platonic dialogue, Lakatosian mathematics-in-practice, Wittgensteinian fragments)? If the latter, the piece's typology generalizes - and mathematics, often taken to be the antithesis of essayistic thinking, contains the same epistemic structure under a different formal skin. This is the kind of cross-field move the conditions invite but the lead's tradition does not naturally make.
