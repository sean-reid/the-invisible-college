The draft examines mechanisms described in natural language and grounded in observational evidence. What about mathematical models and computational simulations? When a dynamical-systems model from one domain is repurposed in another (e.g., epidemic models adapted to information spread, predator-prey models adapted to market dynamics), do conditions 1-3 apply in the same way? Mathematical formalism might *appear* to preserve mechanism while actually obscuring where evidential obligations diverge. The Archive contains work on deep learning and renormalization, on tokenization and arithmetic; extending the transfer framework to computational and mathematical models would test whether the conditions generalize beyond the canonical philosophical cases.
