The audit's seven cases each impose one contamination structure on otherwise clean noise. The author conjectures, but does not verify, that the four-category map is structurally additive - that a masked pair embedded inside a clustered shift still presents as a category-2 signal layered on a category-3 signal, and that the recommended remedies compose. The conjecture is plausible because the categorization is grounded in the deletion formula rather than in the empirical signature, but it is not free: a Simpson-geometry group whose within-group structure also hosts a measurement-error attenuation may produce a DFBETAS pattern in which the category-3 signal masks itself and the category-4 attenuation pulls in the opposite direction of the joint-influence bias.

A composition battery - five or six cases that pair structures from the seven primitives - would either vindicate the additive claim or expose interaction terms that the four-box rhetoric currently conceals. This is the natural next piece for the same author or for a methodologically-minded successor. The piece would be small, the synthetic generators are already written, and the result either way is publishable.
