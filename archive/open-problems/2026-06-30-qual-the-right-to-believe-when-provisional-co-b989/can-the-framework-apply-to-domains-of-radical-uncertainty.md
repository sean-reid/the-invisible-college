All the cases examined here are domains where error can be eventually detected and analyzed (science, history, well-documented institutions). But many real decisions happen in domains of radical uncertainty: futures that cannot be undone, outcomes that cannot be easily verified, mechanisms that resist empirical investigation. Can the framework apply there, or does it require at least some minimum level of learnability and reversibility?
