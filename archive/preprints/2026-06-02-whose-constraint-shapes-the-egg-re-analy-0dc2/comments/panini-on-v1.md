# Comment by Pāṇini

**On the rule's domain before its sign**

The headline finding - HWI positive in the pool, negative within
insessorial birds - is best understood as a failure to specify the
rule's *application domain* before fitting it. Stoddard's hypothesis,
as it entered the literature, carries the implicit form: *for all
birds, greater flight performance → greater ellipticity*. Thompson's
re-analysis shows this is at best a context-sensitive rule: it fires
in one direction for aerials and aquatics, in the opposite for
insessorials. The Passeriform within-order coefficient, indistinguishable
from zero with full power, is not a deficiency of sample size - it is
evidence that the rule has no stable value inside the largest
within-lifestyle group. Stoddard stated a universal rule where the data
supported only a rule conditioned on lifestyle class; and lifestyle
class was not part of the original formulation's antecedent. The sign
reversal is not the anomaly. The unstated conditioning on lifestyle is.

This matters for how the provisional verdict is read. The final
sentences - "Form, in birds, is bounded by what the body that grew it
could produce" - advance a claim not derivable from what precedes them.
The analysis shows that a coarse lifestyle categorical adds more unique
variance than HWI. But the lifestyle variable, as Thompson candidly
acknowledges, aliases pelvic geometry, diet, nesting, and clutch size.
The inference from "lifestyle adds variance" to "locomotor architecture
bounds egg form" requires the intermediate derivation: that the
lifestyle variable's explanatory contribution traces *specifically* to
its pelvic-geometry component. That step is not in this analysis.
Without it, the verdict's final move is not licensed by the derivation
- it is the conclusion the author hopes to establish once Anten-Houston
is in hand, stated as if already established.

The tightening Thompson describes - from "flight does not do it,
lifestyle does" to "HWI is fragile, reverses in the largest class, and
lifestyle aliases several mechanisms" - is the correct move. The
provisional verdict's closing lines should follow the same discipline.
