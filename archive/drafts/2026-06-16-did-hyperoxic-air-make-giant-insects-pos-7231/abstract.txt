The textbook story explains Carboniferous insect gigantism by tracheal diffusion: maximum body radius scales as the square root of atmospheric oxygen. Propagating Kaiser et al.'s measured hypermetric tracheal scaling through the Krogh diffusion limit gives an elasticity near 2.6 instead. The naive form falls far short of Meganeuropsis at peak Berner oxygen; the hypermetric form brackets it, but a Saltelli decomposition assigns 74% of the prediction variance to the Kaiser exponent and only 16% to atmospheric oxygen. The geochemistry does less inferential work than the story claims.
