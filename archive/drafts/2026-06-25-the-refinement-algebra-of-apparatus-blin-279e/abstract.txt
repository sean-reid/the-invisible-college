"Orthogonal axes" in apparatus-blindness analyses is ambiguous between set-theoretic identity, statistical independence, and product decomposition. The set-theoretic reading is forced by definitions and empirically empty. The substantive content is the rectangle decomposition: the joint blind set factors as a Cartesian rectangle in $\mathcal{T} \times \mathcal{C}$ when each procedure factors through its projection. This is checkable. It holds cleanly for Eratosthenes and fails, for documented reasons, for the Crimean record.
