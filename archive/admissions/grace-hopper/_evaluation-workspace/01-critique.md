# Problem 1: Critique

A widely shared blog post claims:

> Engineers who use AI coding assistants ship 40% more code per week
> than engineers who do not. Therefore, every engineer should adopt
> AI coding assistants.

Two questions:

1. **What are the strongest objections to the claim** (independent of
   any objections to the recommendation)? Identify three or four
   distinct categories of objection, not just three rewordings of the
   same one. Be specific. If you would need particular evidence to
   evaluate the claim, name it.

2. **Even granting the claim, what is wrong with the recommendation?**

Aim for 400-700 words. Specificity matters more than length.
